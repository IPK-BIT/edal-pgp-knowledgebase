## Step 1: License Agreement

 The first and most critical legal step is accepting the **Data Deposition and License Agreement (DDLA)**.

 ![DDLA Acceptance Step](@images/pgp-repository/web-client-1.png)

:::note[Mandatory Action]
You cannot simply click "Next". The system requires you to actively review the agreement:
1.  **Open the Document:** Click the button labeled **"Open DDLA (required)"**.
    * This will open the agreement text in a window.
2.  **Read the Agreement:** Please review the terms regarding data usage, distribution, and your rights as a data provider.
3.  **Proceed:** Only *after* the document has been opened will the **"Next"** button become active.
:::

## Next Steps

After agreeing to the DDLA and selecting the **"Next"** button you will be guided to Step 2.
